Windspeed

Although we see a few outliers in the northern hemisphere, there generally tends to be no correlation seen between latitude and windspeed in this dataset. The only correlation one might come to, is there is a more consistent "breeze" of light winds in tropical areas closest to the equator. As with out temperature plot, those gentle breezes are not evenly distributed closest to the equator, but look to extend more into the northern hemisphere. This again may be related to the tilt of the earth's axis when this dataset was compiled.
